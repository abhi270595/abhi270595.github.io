Our history

    We are a rather young company incorporated in September 2018. Initially founded by Darshan Waghela, we started out as an Esports startup but have expanded to three more verticals in our short history.  

    There was a point in time when we questioned whether we had enough funds to complete “Gbait,” our then only project. During this period, someone requested us to build a simple calculator to generate bills for contractors. This opportunity inspired us to take on more community projects, much like freelancers.  

    Over the years, Darshan has brought valuable experience in data science, building advanced AI/ML models that have helped shape and diversify our offerings. We’ve come a long way from being just incorporated freelancers to becoming a company that leverages technology to create meaningful solutions.
